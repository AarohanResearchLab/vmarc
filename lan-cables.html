LAN Cables - CAT5e & CAT6 Data Cables

CAT 6 UTP cables are high performance cables used increasingly for modern computer networking systems. These cables form the bone of modern data transmission in industries and high- rise “intelligent buildings”.

These cables are the backbone of modern data transmission in commercial areas, Industries, Buildings. V-MARC provides a wide range of CAT Cable & Fibre optic Cables that is manufactured using quality tested material. It is a twisted pair cable type designed for high signal integrity. Many such cables are unshielded but some are shielded. Category 5 & 6 has been superseded by the category 5e & 6e specification. This type of cable is often used in structured cabling for computer networks such as Ethernet, and is also used to carry many other signals such as basic voice services. These products are known for high quality features like flexibility, compatibility and durability.
