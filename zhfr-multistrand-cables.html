Product Construction

Standards

IEC 60332-1 & 3, IEC 60754-1 & 2, IS 8130

Conductor

Zero Halogen Flame Retardant cable are made of Bright annealed bare copper of highly Pure Electrolytic Grade with more than 100% conductivity which makes it an excellent conductor of electricity, thus helps in saving energy.

Insulation

ZHFR (Zero Halogen Flame Retardant) wiring cables are made with an imported specially formulated Non-PVC material is a product of most advanced polymer technology. It does not emit highly corrosive halogen acid gases/toxic fumes and there is almost nil smoke. In case of fire the conventional wiring cable will produce more toxic gases which is very harmful for the environment and it will also hamper the rescue operations as the visibility becomes 100 % nil. To combat this type of situations ZHFR (Zero Halogen Flame Retardant) Insulation is very effective.
