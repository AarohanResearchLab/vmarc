FR Multistrand Cables

Product Construction

Standards

IS 694:C1 category, IS 8130 & IS 5831

Conductor

Flame Retardant Cables are made of Pure Electrolytic Grade, bright annealed bare copper with more than 100 % conductivity which makes it an excellent conductor of electricity, thus helps in saving energy.

Insulation

The conductors are insulated with a specially formulated Flame Retardant- FR grade PVC compound having high insulation resistance, oxygen, temperature index and dielectric strength which prevents leakage of electric current, its flame retardant properties minimize the spread of fire which ensuring complete safety from shocks, short circuits and fires.

Colours

V-Marc Multistrand Wires are available in RED, YELLOW, BLUE, BLACK, WHITE & GREEN COLOUR or Any other Colour as per Customer Requirement.

Range

V-Marc Multistrand Wires from 0.50 up to 630 Sq.mm.
