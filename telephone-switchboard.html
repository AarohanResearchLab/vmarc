V-MARC Telephone & Switchboard Cables Designed for Voice Clarity and Elimination of cross talk, are most suitable for indoor use in large office / residential buildings, hotels and industrials complex for interconnection of telephone and intercom system, PBX and in central for interconnection of distribution frames, switching and transmission equipments.

The insulate cores of these cables are twisted to form a pair which is further connected with telephone switching lines to offer noise free audio signal. The structure of these cables makes them easy to connect, protective from interference and capable of rectifying noise while offering clear audio signal.

Product Construction

Standards

Laying of Pairs

The cables (laid up pair) are tightly wrapped with polyester tape applied with an overlap of 30%. This facility is not available in single pair.
