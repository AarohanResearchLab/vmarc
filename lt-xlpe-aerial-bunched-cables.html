LT XLPE Aerial Bunched Cables

Aerial Bunched Cable (ABC), I.I K.V. volt Grade, are used for disturbing power to individual consumers by utility service providers like Electricity Boards, electricity distributing authorities. The cable is kept supported on poles. The line is taped intermittently form any required position which enables it to be used in urban as well as rural areas.

Conductor

Phase or lighting Conductor: hard drawn Aluminium H2 or H4 grade as per IS: 8130, Messenger conductor: Aluminium Silica and Magnesium Alloy used after treatment as per IS: 398 (Part4)

Insulation

XLPE / P.E. applied by extrusion process.

Identification Of Phase

By providing ridges on the insulation of Phase Conductors. One ridge for 1st phase, 2 and 3 ridges for 2nd and 3rd phases respectively. Neutral can have 4 ridges, if desired.

Colour Of Insulation

Because the cable remains exposed to environments & sunlight (Ultraviolet radiation) the insulation is mixed with a small amount of carbon back to stop deterioration of polythene due to UV radiation.

Assembling

The phase conductor along with the lighting conductor (where applicable) is twisted around messenger conductor.

Construction

The phase conductor can be of single phase and also of three phases. Lighting conductor can be also incorporated to give supply to the street lighting. Messenger conductor supports the weight of the cable and keeps the assembly strung under tension. The phase conductor is made of concentrically stranded Aluminum wires having 7 or 19 wires. The messenger conductor is made of specially treated silica, Magnesium Aluminum alloy having 7 wires. The phase conductor and lighting conductor where applicable are insulated with XLPE/P.E. compound. The messenger conductor, as per customerâ€™s choice is kept either bare or insulated. The phase conductor along with lighting conductor (where applicable) are twisted around the messenger conductor (insulated or bare) and left as such. Sometimes as a special care a neutral conductor is also provided separately if desired, by the user. Generally messenger acts as well as neutral.

This type of construction has the following additional advantages:

  • Cables are lighter in weight
  • Easy to install
  • Can be installed on poles, on walls etc.
  • Easy to make termination and branch off joints on live wire as well.
  • Protection against power theft
