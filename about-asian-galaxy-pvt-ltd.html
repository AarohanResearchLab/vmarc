About V-Marc India Limited

V-Marc India Limited was founded in the year 1996 as a manufacturing and marketing firm dealing in electrical wires & cables for modern needs with brand name “V-Marc”.

We manufacture various types of industrial quality Wires & Cables such as FR, FR-LSH, ZHFR, PVC Single & Multicore Copper/Aluminum Cables, XLPE/PVC insulated Flat Cables, Co-axial Cables, Rubber Cables,LAN Cables, LT Power/Control Cable, HT Cables, Specialty Cables and many more. Our range of products caters specific needs of our clients – meeting all necessary quality standards.

Customer’s Satisfaction is Our Goal

V-marc derives its strength from its customers. The growth of the latter is the first stepping stone to the growth of the company and hence customer’s satisfaction is our prime objective. V-Marc has earned the trust and reputation of millions in India by winning our customer’s confidence. Several Lakh kilometers of Wires & Cables have been manufactured and are currently in operation in different parts of India.

V-marc Wires & Cables are preferred choice for their working in Power Plants, Distribution Systems, Heavy Industries, and various other utilities. The organization is also committed to comply with all the applicable environment, health & safety legislation and all the requirements of interested parties. We are poised for a big growth both in quantity & quality. Our commitment to the nation to produce the best in quality and deliver optimum satisfaction to our customers. We also strive to ensure awareness among employees, society, interested parties about environment protection, minimization of waste products, wise use of energy, water and other natural resources so as to make this world a better place to live in. That’s the goal of our company.
